FAQ

What about pallet height and weight ratings?

Those vary widely by design (stringer vs block), material, and manufacturer. Use the Pallet Weight Calculator and confirm ratings with your supplier.

Do you support inches?

Yes. Open the calculator in inches using the US link or switch units inside the tool.

Are there other sizes?

Yes. Industry and regional variants exist. You can enter any custom size in the calculator to plan your load.
