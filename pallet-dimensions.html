Pallet sizes vary by region and application. Below is a table of common pallet dimensions used around the world. Dimensions are shown as length × width. Heights vary depending on construction and material.

Region/Standard Dimensions (inches) Equivalent (mm) Notes
North America (48 × 40) 48 × 40 1219 × 1016 Most common U.S. grocery pallet
European (EUR/EPAL) 47.2 × 31.5 1200 × 800 Also known as EUR‑1; fits EU racking
U.K. / Australian 45.9 × 45.9 1165 × 1165 Square pallet used in Oceania
Asia (JIS) 43.3 × 43.3 1100 × 1100 Standard in Japan and parts of Asia
Half pallet 24 × 40 610 × 1016 Often used for in-store displays
Quarter pallet 24 × 20 610 × 508 Small format for lightweight goods
