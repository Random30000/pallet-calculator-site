Quick guide + estimator

How many pallets fit in a 20ft container?

Short answer for single‑stack loading (most common): 11 Euro pallets (1200×800), 10 standard pallets (1200×1000), and about 10 US pallets (48×40). Exact capacity varies with loading pattern, clearances, and policies.

Why the numbers vary

  • Different loading patterns (straight, pin‑wheel, mixed) change how leftover space is used.
  • Door clearance, pallet overhang, and safety margins reduce theoretical counts.
  • Double‑stacking or higher ceilings change totals; use the full calculator for stack height/weight limits.
