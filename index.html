FAQ

Are the results exact?

They are planning estimates. Real‑world fit depends on pallet overhang, packaging tolerances, and safety policies.

Which standards are supported?

EU (1200×800, 1200×1000), US (48×40 / 1219×1016), AU (1165×1165), JP (1100×1100). You can also input any custom size.

Can I share the results?

Yes. The calculator creates a shareable URL with your inputs so teammates can reproduce the result.
